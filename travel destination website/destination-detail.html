About This Destination

The Maldives is a tropical paradise located in the Indian Ocean, consisting of 26 atolls and over 1,000 coral islands. Known for its pristine white sandy beaches, crystal-clear turquoise waters, and vibrant marine life, the Maldives offers an unparalleled escape from everyday life.

Whether you're seeking relaxation in luxurious overwater bungalows, exciting water sports adventures, or romantic sunset cruises, the Maldives delivers on every front. The warm hospitality, exquisite cuisine, and breathtaking natural beauty make it a bucket-list destination for travelers worldwide.

Best Time to Visit

The Maldives enjoys a tropical climate year-round, with two distinct seasons:

Dry Season (November - April)

Perfect weather with minimal rainfall, ideal for diving and beach activities. This is peak tourist season with slightly higher prices.

Wet Season (May - October)

More affordable rates with occasional rain showers. Still warm and great for surfing. Fewer tourists mean more privacy.
